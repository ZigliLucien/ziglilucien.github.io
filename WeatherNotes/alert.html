Special Weather Statement issued February 17 at 10:21PM CST by NWS Paducah KY

Strong south winds will gust 30-40 mph at times late this evening and into the overnight hours over most of southeast Missouri, and generally to the west of Interstate 57 in southern Illinois. The gusty winds should diminish some before sunrise Wednesday. The strong wind gusts will blow around unsecured objects and could make driving difficult on east west oriented roadways.
