Special Weather Statement issued December 9 at 2:23PM CST by NWS Paducah KY

Southwest winds will increase through the night, with sustained winds 15-25 mph and gusts 25-40 mph expected. As a cold front passes, the winds will gradually shift to the west early Wednesday and then become northwest by afternoon. The strongest winds are expected between midnight and noon, and some gusts over 40 mph are possible. Unsecured outdoor decorations and items may become displaced by the gusty winds.
