Frost Advisory issued October 15 at 2:08PM CDT until October 16 at 8:00AM CDT by NWS Paducah KY

* WHAT...Clearing skies, diminishing winds, and low temperatures in the lower to middle 30s will result in frost formation. * WHERE...All of southern Illinois, southwest Indiana, western Kentucky, and southeast Missouri. * WHEN...From 1 AM CDT /2 AM EDT/ to 8 AM CDT /9 AM EDT/ Wednesday. * IMPACTS...Frost could harm sensitive outdoor vegetation. Sensitive outdoor plants may be killed if left uncovered.
