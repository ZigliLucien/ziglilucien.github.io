Special Weather Statement issued July 6 at 11:32AM CDT by NWS Paducah KY

At 1134 am isolated thunderstorms were beginning to develop from near Doniphan, MO northeast to Mt. Vernon, IN. Additional development is likely in this region through early afternoon and the activity will slowly move to the east at 10 to 15 mph. Conditions are favorable for a few storms to produce gusty winds and the strongest storms could become briefly severe with damaging wind gusts to 60 mph over the next few hours.
