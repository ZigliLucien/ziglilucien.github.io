Freeze Watch issued April 6 at 7:49PM CDT until April 8 at 9:00AM CDT by NWS Paducah KY

* WHAT...Sub-freezing temperatures as low as 25 possible. * WHERE...Portions of southern Illinois, southwest Indiana, western Kentucky, and southeast Missouri. * WHEN...From late Monday night through Tuesday morning. * IMPACTS...Frost and freeze conditions could kill crops, other sensitive vegetation and possibly damage unprotected outdoor plumbing.
