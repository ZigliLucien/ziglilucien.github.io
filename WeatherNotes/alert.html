Special Weather Statement issued May 15 at 6:32PM CDT by NWS Paducah KY

Gusty Winds Associated With Decaying Showers... Wind gusts of 40 to 50 mph are possible for a period of time early this evening due to a decaying area of showers. Those in high profile vehicles may encounter some difficult driving conditions as well. A few tree limbs may be blown down. Unsecure objects may be blown around.
