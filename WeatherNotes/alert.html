Winter Weather Advisory issued December 1 at 10:52AM CST until December 2 at 6:00AM CST by NWS Paducah KY

* WHAT...A mix of snow, sleet and freezing rain/freezing drizzle. Total snow and sleet accumulations up to two inches and ice accumulations around a light glaze. Cold ground temperatures may lead to very slick conditions. * WHERE...Southern Illinois, southeast Missouri, southwest Indiana and western Kentucky. * WHEN...From 6 PM this evening to 6 AM CST Tuesday. * IMPACTS...Be prepared for roads, and especially bridges and overpasses to become slick and hazardous. Plan on slippery road conditions.
