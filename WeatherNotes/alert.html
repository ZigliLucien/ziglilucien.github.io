Special Weather Statement issued April 13 at 2:15AM CDT by NWS Paducah KY

South winds will increase today becoming sustained 15 to 25 mph this afternoon along with gusts of 30 to 40 mph. The strongest gusts are expected to occur across southeast Missouri into southwest Illinois. Gusty winds may blow around unsecured objects. A few tree limbs may also be blown down. High profile vehicles may encounter some driving difficulty, especially on east-west roads.
