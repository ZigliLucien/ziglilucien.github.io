Special Weather Statement issued December 13 at 2:52PM CST by NWS Paducah KY

A mixture of freezing drizzle or light snow is possible across portions of the region this afternoon and evening. As temperatures fall below freezing, scattered slick spots may develop on many surfaces. Use caution if travelling or outside the rest of today.
